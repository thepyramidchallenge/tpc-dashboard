### E1 — Online challenge → $99 one-off report attach rate  ★ north-star
- Status: PROPOSED
- Domain: pricing, platform
- Updated: 2026-06-27
- Question: does measurement willingness-to-pay transfer to at-home online? (H2)
- Metric: **% of online-challenge completers who buy the $99 report.**
  Secondary: **$99 → $198 upgrade rate** (Q4).
- Threshold (read against cost, see H3): ~12% attach ≈ self-sustain at the future
  maintenance cost; ~19% at today's lean cost. Track the trajectory, not a single
  pass/fail — even 5–10% early is informative.
- Method: run one online challenge (e.g. alongside Season 3), reuse the existing
  Sheets→Affinity report pipeline to produce the report off the **online** result.
  Frame the online report honestly as **practice / formative assessment** (not a
  certified ranking) to protect the brand.

- Updates: H2, H3, D1, D4

### E2 — Season 3 full-package bundle → 6-month renewal rate
- Status: PROPOSED
- Domain: pricing
- Updated: 2026-06-27
- Question: will near-free **seeded** members pay the real $198 to continue? (the
  cleanest trust-transfer signal, H2)
- Metric: **% of full-package buyers who renew the online tier at $198** when
  their bundled 6 months end. Watch **activation** too (do seeded members even log
  in? — near-free acquisition risks dead accounts).
- Threshold: a renewal rate materially above zero is the proof that the bundled
  tier delivered real value; treat bundled members as **seeded**, never as
  "validated paid members" until they renew.
- Method: bundle the **6-month** tier (D2) so the renewal lands inside the
  learning window; tag bundle-seeded vs standalone-acquired members separately.
- Result: TBD
- Updates: H2, D1, D2
